Continua is a Management System Consultancy offering support to energy clients in the USA. With a focus upon Quality, Health & Safety and Environment (QHSE) Continua aims to provide simple scalable solutions to meet client requirements. With the option of working with other independent professionals and small businesses Continua can draw upon a wide range of resources to meet project objectives which include;

  • ISO Certification Pursuit: Quality, Health & Safety and Environment (QHSE)
  • Process Implementation and Improvement Planning
  • Internal Audit and Gap Assessment
  • Strategic Risk Assessment
  • SWOT Workshops
  • Stakeholder Assessment
  • Management Review Facilitation
“In 2016 I established Continua Solutions to ensure that I could continue to work with energy clients and trusted QHSE colleagues regardless of where this crazy market takes us! Since the downturn we are all finding new ways of working; we are more lean and we are, perhaps, more innovative. Continua embraces these changes and aims to play its part in ensuring that industry experience and knowledge is neither lost, nor reduced, during this time. To this end collaboration, networking and continual learning are at the heart of my company. It's an exciting time to be in the energy industry and I'm proud to work with clients and colleagues who are bringing a wealth of good practice and lessons learned into the new landscape of energy production.”
LYNNE
