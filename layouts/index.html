Continua is a Management System Consultancy offering support to energy clients in the USA. With a focus upon Quality, Health & Safety and Environment (QHSE) Continua aims to provide simple scalable solutions to meet client requirements. With the option of working with other independent professionals and small businesses Continua can draw upon a wide range of resources to meet project objectives which include;

  • ISO Certification Pursuit: Quality, Health & Safety and Environment (QHSE)
  • Process Implementation and Improvement Planning
  • Internal Audit and Gap Assessment
  • Strategic Risk Assessment
  • SWOT Workshops
  • Stakeholder Assessment
  • Management Review Facilitation
“I take pride in working with my clients to build or to improve management systems that go beyond a “rubber stamp exercise”.

We work together to implement processes that drive meaningful improvements for their organizations.

Every client is unique. Every organization faces different challenges, has different opportunities, different resources and different requirements from their stakeholders. I do my best to really listen to my clients so that I can create bespoke solutions that meet their specific needs, budget and timeline.

Little projects, big projects, complex support or a simple helping hand… they all have one thing in common; they provide a tool kit for the continual improvement of the processes that matter the most to my clients.”
